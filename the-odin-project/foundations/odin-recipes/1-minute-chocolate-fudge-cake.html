1 MINUTE CHOCOLATE FUDGE CAKE

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 1 minutea

Total Time: 6 minutes

Serves: 1

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp dark chocolate
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • ¼ cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • ⅛ tsp baking powder
  • chocolate chips to top
  • vanilla icecream to serve

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate and butter in the microwave
  2. Add in the sugar and yogurt and mix well
  3. Lastly fold in all the dry ingredients and top some dark chocolate chips
  4. Cook in microwave for 1 minute, add some vanilla icecream and serve hot.
