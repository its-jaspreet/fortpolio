4 INGREDIENT CHOCOLAVA CAKE

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Serves: 1

Ingredients:

  • 10 oreo cookies
  • ¼ cup hot milk
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 3 chunks of chocolate

Instructions:

  1. Put oreos in a bowl amd add hot milk to it. Let it sit for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Add baking soda and mix everything. Break the lumps if any using the whisk.
  3. In a greased and dusted mould add the batter till the mould is half full. Add chocolate chunks in the middle and cover with some more batter.
  4. Bake/ airfry for 8-10 minutes at 180°C. Serve hot with some icecream and ENJOY!
