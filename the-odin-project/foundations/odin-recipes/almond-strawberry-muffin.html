ALMOND STRAWBERRY MUFFINS

Prep Time: 45 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 75 minutes

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • 1 + ½ cup all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ cup fresh strawberries, diced
  • ¼ cup almond flakes

Instructions:

  1. Pre heat the oven to 180°C. Line a muffin mould with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, combine oil, sugar and vanilla extract.
  3. Add the egg and beat well. Mix in the almond milk.
  4. Add flour and baking powder to the wet ingredients. Do not over mix- mix only until well combined.
  5. Carefully fold in the strawberries.
  6. Using an ice-cream scoop, scoop equal amount of batter into the moulds. Sprinkle the almond flakes on top
  7. Bake at 180°C for 25-30 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
